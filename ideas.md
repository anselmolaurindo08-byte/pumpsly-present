**Design Movement**: Cyberpunk Neon Futurism

**Core Principles**:
- High-contrast neon elements on deep black backgrounds
- Sharp geometric shapes with glowing edges
- Kinetic energy through animated gradients and light trails
- Asymmetric layouts that break traditional grid systems

**Color Philosophy**: 
Electric neon palette inspired by Tokyo nightscapes and cyberpunk aesthetics. Neon green (#00FF41) represents growth and money, electric purple (#8B5CF6) conveys innovation and crypto culture, while cyan (#06B6D4) adds technological sophistication. The near-black background (#0A0A0F) makes neon colors pop with maximum intensity.

**Layout Paradigm**: 
Diagonal split-screen compositions with overlapping layers. Each slide uses angular divisions rather than horizontal sections, creating dynamic tension. Content flows along diagonal axes, with key information positioned at intersection points of these angles.

**Signature Elements**:
- Glowing hexagonal grid patterns that pulse with data
- Neon-outlined cards with blur effects and inner glow
- Animated scan lines that sweep across sections
- Holographic gradient overlays on text and backgrounds

**Interaction Philosophy**: 
Every interaction should feel like manipulating a holographic interface. Hover states trigger neon glow intensification, buttons emit light pulses on click, and transitions use glitch effects and digital dissolves. Scrolling between slides feels like navigating through layers of a 3D space.

**Animation**:
- Entrance: Elements materialize with neon draw-in effects (stroke animation)
- Scroll: Parallax depth with foreground elements moving faster than background
- Hover: Glow intensity increases, subtle scale transforms, and shadow bloom
- Transitions: Glitch distortion combined with fade, creating digital artifact effects
- Idle: Subtle pulsing of neon elements, floating particles in background

**Typography System**:
- Display: Orbitron Bold (80px) for main headlines - geometric, futuristic
- Subheadings: Space Grotesk Medium (32px) - modern, technical
- Body: Inter Regular (18px) - clean readability
- Accent: Monospace (Roboto Mono) for numbers, stats, and technical data
- All text has subtle text-shadow glow matching neon color scheme

**Design Movement**: Brutalist Maximalism

**Core Principles**:
- Raw, unpolished aesthetics with intentional roughness
- Oversized typography that dominates the viewport
- Dense information layouts with controlled chaos
- Bold color blocks that clash intentionally

**Color Philosophy**:
Aggressive color combinations that demand attention. Primary palette uses stark black (#000000) and pure white (#FFFFFF) as base, with violent accent colors: warning orange (#FF6B00), danger red (#FF0055), and toxic green (#39FF14). Colors are used in large, unapologetic blocks that create visual tension and hierarchy through sheer force.

**Layout Paradigm**:
Anti-grid system where elements are positioned with intentional awkwardness. Text boxes overlap images, sections have uneven margins, and whitespace is deliberately uncomfortable. Each slide is a collision of elements fighting for attention, yet organized through scale hierarchy and color weight.

**Signature Elements**:
- Massive, cropped typography that bleeds off screen edges
- Thick, irregular borders (8-12px) around content blocks
- Distressed textures and grain overlays
- Oversized arrows and geometric shapes as navigation cues

**Interaction Philosophy**:
Interactions are abrupt and honest. No smooth easing - everything uses linear or stepped animations. Clicks produce harsh transitions, hovers trigger instant color inversions, and scrolling snaps aggressively. The interface feels like physical machinery - heavy, direct, and unforgiving.

**Animation**:
- Entrance: Hard cuts with brief static/noise bursts
- Scroll: Snap scrolling with mechanical precision, no smooth easing
- Hover: Instant color inversion or 180° rotation
- Transitions: Jump cuts, scale pops, and position snaps
- Idle: Occasional glitch artifacts, scanline interference

**Typography System**:
- Display: Druk Wide Bold (120px) - ultra-condensed, overwhelming presence
- Subheadings: Archivo Black (48px) - heavy, authoritative
- Body: IBM Plex Mono (16px) - technical, dense
- Accent: Bebas Neue (64px) for callouts and numbers
- Mix of all-caps and sentence case for visual rhythm disruption

**Design Movement**: Liquid Gradient Modernism

**Core Principles**:
- Flowing, organic shapes that morph and blend
- Soft depth through layered transparency
- Continuous motion creating living interfaces
- Harmonious color transitions that guide the eye

**Color Philosophy**:
Ethereal gradient spectrum that evokes digital fluidity and market dynamics. Base gradients flow from deep purple (#6B21A8) through vibrant pink (#EC4899) to electric blue (#3B82F6) and mint green (#10B981). Each slide features unique gradient combinations that represent different emotional states: purple-pink for excitement, blue-cyan for trust, green-yellow for growth. Gradients are never static - they shift subtly with scroll position and time.

**Layout Paradigm**:
Fluid grid system where content sits in organic, rounded containers that appear to float in space. Layouts use circular and blob-shaped masks, with content flowing around these forms. Vertical rhythm is established through varying blob sizes, creating a sense of natural hierarchy without rigid structure.

**Signature Elements**:
- Morphing gradient blobs that shift shape on scroll
- Glassmorphic cards with backdrop blur and subtle borders
- Floating particles that follow cursor movement
- Gradient mesh backgrounds that animate continuously

**Interaction Philosophy**:
Interactions feel liquid and responsive, like touching water. Hovers create ripple effects, clicks send out expanding rings of color, and scrolling causes elements to flow like currents. The interface breathes - elements gently scale and shift even at rest, creating a sense of organic life.

**Animation**:
- Entrance: Fade-in with elastic scale, elements "inflate" into view
- Scroll: Smooth parallax with different layers moving at varied speeds, gradient shifts
- Hover: Gentle lift (translateY: -8px) with glow expansion, ripple effect emanates
- Transitions: Morph transitions where shapes blend into next slide's shapes
- Idle: Continuous gradient rotation (360° over 20s), subtle floating motion

**Typography System**:
- Display: Clash Display Variable (72px) - elegant curves, high contrast
- Subheadings: Plus Jakarta Sans SemiBold (28px) - rounded, friendly
- Body: Inter Variable (17px) - excellent readability, subtle warmth
- Accent: DM Sans Medium (20px) for stats and highlights
- Text uses gradient fills for headlines, creating visual cohesion with background
